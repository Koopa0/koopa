Privacy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. About this site

koopa0.dev is one person's public website. It publishes my articles, build logs, TILs, and project notes. There is nothing here to sign up for and no public product or hosted offering behind these pages.

2. Server logs

Public pages are served by ordinary web infrastructure that records standard request logs (such as IP address, request path, response status, and timestamp). I use these to keep the site running and to investigate errors. They are not sold or shared.

3. No signup or sign-in for visitors

The public site does not offer signup or a visitor login. There is nothing to register for, and I do not ask visitors to provide personal information in order to read what is published here.

4. No analytics or third-party trackers

As of the date above, this site does not load Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, PostHog, Hotjar, or any similar tracking script. If that ever changes, I will update this page first and call it out explicitly here rather than ship it silently.

5. Cookies

Reading public pages does not require setting cookies in your browser. If a future feature ever needs one for the public site, it will be described in this section before it ships.

6. Private surfaces are private

I run private admin and authoring surfaces behind this site to manage my own work. Those are not part of the public site, are not linked from public pages, and are not available for guest access, demos, or sign-up. Any login or admin screen you might guess at is for me, not for visitors.

7. External links

Contact and project links may send you to third-party destinations such as GitHub, X, LinkedIn, or email. Once you click through, the privacy practices of those destinations apply — I have no control over what they log.

8. Updates

If anything on this page changes, the "Last updated" date above will move. There is no separate notification mechanism — this page is the canonical record.

9. Contact

Questions, corrections, or removal requests — email contact@koopa0.dev .
